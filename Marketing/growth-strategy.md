# Growth Strategy - Deets

**Goal:** Sustainable, organic growth to 100,000+ downloads in Year 1
**Philosophy:** Quality over quantity, community over ads, value over viral
**Budget:** Minimal ($500-1,000 marketing spend)

---

## Growth Framework

### The Pyramid

```
                    /\
                   /  \
                  / 10 \    Evangelists (create UGC, refer actively)
                 /______\
                /        \
               /   100    \  Power Users (daily use, give feedback)
              /____________\
             /              \
            /     1,000      \  Active Users (weekly use)
           /_______________  \
          /                   \
         /      10,000         \ Casual Users (monthly use)
        /_______________________\
       /                         \
      /         100,000           \ Downloads (tried once)
     /___________________________  \
```

**Strategy:** Build from the top down
- Start with 10 evangelists (beta testers, early adopters)
- Grow to 100 power users who love and use daily
- They bring 1,000 active users through word of mouth
- Active users attract casual users
- Casual users drive downloads

## User Acquisition Channels

### Tier 1: Owned & Earned (Primary Focus)

#### 1. App Store Optimization (ASO)
**Effort:** High upfront, low ongoing
**Cost:** $0
**Expected Impact:** 30-40% of organic downloads

**Optimization Areas:**

**App Name:**
- Primary: "Deets - Business Card Scanner"
- Keywords: Privacy, OCR, Contacts
- Character limit: Maximize without keyword stuffing

**Subtitle:**
- "Meet once. Remember always."
- Or: "Privacy-first contact management"

**Keywords:**
- Primary: business card scanner, contact scanner, OCR, networking
- Secondary: privacy, local storage, card reader, vcard
- Long-tail: conference networking, business card management

**Description:**
- First 2 lines critical (above fold)
- Clear problem â†’ solution â†’ benefit
- Bullet points for scannability
- Include key features
- End with privacy commitment
- Use all 4,000 characters

**Screenshots:**
- 6-8 screens showing workflow
- Text overlays explaining value
- Before/after comparisons
- Highlight privacy visually
- Show on different devices
- Update seasonally

**Preview Video:**
- 30 seconds max
- Show scan â†’ edit â†’ save
- Emphasize speed and ease
- Voiceover or text (no audio required)
- Hook in first 3 seconds

**Reviews & Ratings:**
- Target: 4.5+ stars, 100+ reviews in first month
- Strategy: Ask happy users at peak satisfaction moment
- Respond to ALL reviews within 24 hours
- Fix issues mentioned in negative reviews

**Localization:**
- Start: English
- Phase 2: Spanish, French, German
- Phase 3: Japanese, Chinese, Portuguese

**Categories:**
- Primary: Productivity
- Secondary: Business
- Consider: Utilities

**Regular Updates:**
- Ship updates every 2-4 weeks
- "What's New" showcases improvements
- Signals active development
- Improves store ranking

**Expected Results:**
- Month 1: 100-200 organic downloads/week
- Month 3: 300-500 organic downloads/week
- Month 6: 500-1,000 organic downloads/week

## User Retention Strategy

**The harsh truth:** Acquisition is nothing without retention.

### Retention Metrics to Track

**Day 1:** 70%+ (did they scan a card?)
**Day 7:** 40%+ (did they come back?)
**Day 30:** 20%+ (are they active users?)
**Day 90:** 10%+ (are they retained long-term?)

### Retention Tactics

#### Week 1: Onboarding
- Clear first-run experience
- Encourage scanning first card immediately
- Set expectations (privacy, features)
- Quick win: Save to Contacts

#### Week 2-4: Habit Formation
- Email: "5 features you might have missed"
- Push notification: "Scan more cards?" (if enabled)
- In-app tips during usage
- Highlight value (cards scanned, time saved)

#### Month 2-3: Power User Activation
- Showcase advanced features
- Invite to beta program
- Request feedback
- Offer to feature their workflow

#### Month 4+: Long-term Engagement
- Regular updates (stay top of mind)
- Community involvement (Discord)
- Feature requests implementation
- User spotlights

## Red Flags to Watch For

### Growth Problems:

**High downloads, low activation:**
- Problem: App doesn't deliver on promise
- Fix: Improve onboarding, set expectations

**High activation, low retention:**
- Problem: Not enough ongoing value
- Fix: Add features, improve UX, email engagement

**Organic traffic declining:**
- Problem: ASO or algorithm changes
- Fix: Update screenshots, optimize keywords, ship updates

**High CAC (if paying for ads):**
- Problem: Wrong targeting or poor creative
- Fix: Refine audience, test new ads, focus on organic

**Negative reviews increasing:**
- Problem: Bugs, UX issues, unmet expectations
- Fix: Respond to reviews, fix issues, communicate better

## Email Best Practices

### Writing Style
- Conversational and friendly
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)
- Use emojis sparingly (adds personality)
- Always include a clear CTA
- Personal sign-off from founder

### Formatting
- Use line breaks liberally
- Bold important points
- Bullet lists for scannability
- Include relevant links
- Mobile-optimized (most people read on phone)

### Timing
- Welcome: Immediate
- Feature tips: Day 3-7
- Feedback request: Day 14-21
- Newsletter: Monthly (first Tuesday)
- Updates: As shipped

### Metrics to Track
- Open rate (target: 25-35%)
- Click rate (target: 5-10%)
- Reply rate (aim for conversation)
- Unsubscribe rate (<0.5% acceptable)

### A/B Test
- Subject lines
- Send times
- Email length
- CTA placement
- Tone (casual vs. professional)

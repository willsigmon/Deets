Frequently Asked Questions

Is Deets really free?

Yes. No subscription, no in-app purchases, no hidden costs. We built Deets to solve a problem we had, and we want everyone to benefit.

How accurate is the scanning?

Deets uses Apple's VisionKit framework, the same technology that powers text recognition across iOS. It's incredibly accurate with clear business cards. You can always review and edit before saving.

Can I sync contacts across devices?

Currently, all contacts are stored locally on your device for maximum privacy. We're exploring optional iCloud sync for future versions while maintaining our privacy-first approach.

What happens to my data?

It stays on your device. Period. We don't have servers, we don't collect analytics, and we can't access your contacts even if we wanted to.

Can I export my contacts?

Absolutely. Save individual contacts to your iOS Contacts app, or export as vCard files to use anywhere. Your data is never locked in.

What languages are supported?

Deets currently supports English, with more languages coming soon. The OCR works with business cards in any language that uses Latin characters.

How do I get support?

Email us at support@deetsapp.com or tweet @DeetsApp. We read every message and typically respond within 24 hours.

## Common Questions & Answers

**Q: Can we get exclusive early access?**
A: Yes! For significant coverage opportunities, we can provide early TestFlight access before public release.

**Q: Do you have user testimonials?**
A: Yes, we can provide verified testimonials from beta testers and early users. Contact us for specific use cases (sales, entrepreneurs, etc.).

**Q: Can we interview actual users?**
A: Possibly. We can connect you with users who've agreed to share their stories (with their permission).

**Q: What metrics can you share?**
A: We can share aggregated, anonymized data like: total downloads, App Store rating, number of cards scanned, etc. We NEVER share individual user data.

**Q: Can we get a demo?**
A: Absolutely. We can do a live screen-share demo or provide a pre-recorded video.

**Q: Is there a story behind the name?**
A: Yes! "Deets" is slang for "details" - as in, "give me the deets." Perfect for an app that captures contact details.

**Q: What's your business model?**
A: Currently, Deets is free with no monetization. We may explore ethical premium features in the future, but will never sell user data.

**Q: Are you open source?**
A: Not yet, but we're considering it. We want to be transparent about our privacy claims and allow community audits.

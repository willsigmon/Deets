## Brand Voice Checklist

When writing promotional copy, ensure:
- [ ] Confident, not arrogant ("We protect privacy" vs "We're the best")
- [ ] Human, not corporate ("No BS" vs "Optimal data governance")
- [ ] Clear, not clever (Direct value prop vs wordplay)
- [ ] Honest, not hyperbolic ("Private" vs "Military-grade secure")
- [ ] Inclusive, not exclusive ("For professionals" vs "For elite networkers")

## KEYWORD VARIATIONS TO AVOID

DON'T USE (Violates Apple Guidelines):
❌ "best business card scanner" (superlative claim)
❌ "free scanner" (price keyword, misleading)
❌ "#1 card scanner" (ranking claim)
❌ "iPhone scanner" (device name not allowed)
❌ "iOS scanner" (platform name not allowed)
❌ "app" (redundant in App Store)

DON'T USE (Low relevance / off-topic):
❌ "CRM" (enterprise focus, not our market)
❌ "social" (we're not a social app)
❌ "digital business card" (we scan physical cards)
❌ "QR code" (not a primary feature)
❌ "lead generation" (sales focus, not our angle)

## A/B TESTING PLAN

### Test 1: Privacy Focus vs Feature Focus

**Current (Privacy)**:
business card,scanner,contact,networking,OCR,vcard,export,offline,private,cards

**Alternative (Feature)**:
business card,scanner,contact,reader,organizer,export,vcard,OCR,manager,wallet

Hypothesis: Privacy keywords attract quality users; feature keywords drive volume
Test Duration: 90 days
Measure: Downloads, conversion rate, retention

### Test 2: Technical vs Consumer Keywords

**Current (Mixed)**:
business card,scanner,contact,networking,OCR,vcard,export,offline,private,cards

**Alternative (Consumer)**:
business card,scanner,contact,networking,reader,organizer,cards,keeper,holder,manager

Hypothesis: Consumer terms (reader, organizer) have higher volume than technical terms (OCR, vcard)
Test Duration: 90 days
Measure: Impressions, keyword ranking

## KEYWORD PERFORMANCE TRACKING

### Metrics to Monitor (App Store Connect Analytics)

1. **Impressions by Keyword**
   - Which keywords are showing our app?
   - Are target keywords generating impressions?

2. **Conversion Rate by Keyword**
   - Which keywords lead to downloads?
   - Are privacy keywords converting better?

3. **Keyword Ranking**
   - Position for each target keyword
   - Movement over time (trending up/down)

4. **Search Traffic %**
   - What % of downloads come from search?
   - Goal: 40%+ from organic search

### Monthly Review Checklist
- [ ] Check keyword rankings in App Store Connect
- [ ] Review conversion rates by keyword
- [ ] Identify new competitor keywords
- [ ] Test keyword variations (if applicable)
- [ ] Update description if needed (no app update required)

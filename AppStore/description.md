## Pricing

**Model**: Paid (one-time purchase)
**Price Tier**: $2.99 USD (Tier 3)

**Rationale**:
- No subscription (user-friendly, privacy-focused)
- Low enough for impulse purchase
- High enough to signal quality
- No ads or in-app purchases

**Alternative Pricing**:
- Free with IAP ($4.99 unlock): Allows trial, but complicates privacy messaging
- $4.99 upfront (Tier 5): Higher barrier to entry
- Free (ad-supported): Violates privacy principles

**Chosen**: $2.99 paid upfront

## Review Request Strategy

### When to Request Reviews

**In-App Prompts** (use `SKStoreReviewController`):
1. After 5th successful scan
2. After first export to Contacts
3. After 10th saved contact
4. After 30 days of usage (light user)

**Frequency Limit**: Maximum 3 requests per 365 days (Apple enforced)

### Review Response Strategy

**Positive Reviews (5 stars)**:
- Thank user personally
- Highlight privacy benefits
- Encourage sharing with colleagues

**Negative Reviews (1-3 stars)**:
- Respond within 24 hours
- Offer support email for issues
- Acknowledge feedback for feature requests
- Never argue or be defensive

**Feature Requests**:
- Thank user for suggestion
- Add to public roadmap if reasonable
- Explain privacy/scope constraints if not feasible
